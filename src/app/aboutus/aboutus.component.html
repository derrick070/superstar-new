About Us
Our journey began in 1989, where we began providing services for refrigerators and conditioners in Sakinaka, Mumbai. Our service based company expanded to product manufacturing in 1994. We started manufacturing deep freezers and water coolers to provide high class products to commercial companies. Our manufacturing unit shifted from Sakinaka to Latur to expand our products range and reach small and high end business. Today we are one of the leading producers of cooling equipments in our product lineup. As a company our motto has always been to produce high-quality produts at equivalent prices without compromising performance. Our products have a reach from Latur to many popular cities of Maharashtra and Karnataka. Our goal is to improve our products, lineup and expand our reach to more cities.
